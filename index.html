Design philosophy

I design clear, honest objects, balancing technical rigor with everyday sensitivity. I’m guided by materials, proportions, and functionality: clean forms, precise details, and discreet technology.

I collect vintage tech and experiment with 3D printing and smart components to thoughtfully integrate the digital.

My goal is to create products that are legible, repairable, and calm — designs that are understood at a glance and improve with use.
